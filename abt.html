Made to dominate the runway

Based in the vibrant heart of London, Elentra stands as a pinnacle of excellence in the modeling industry,
embodying sophistication, diversity, and unparalleled talent. With a roster of 25 exceptional models, Elentra
curates experiences that transcend conventional standards, fostering collaborations with 12 esteemed
fashion brands to shape the future of the fashion landscape.

At Elentra, we believe in the transformative power of diversity, embracing individuals from all walks of life,
backgrounds, and cultures. Our agency serves as a platform for these diverse voices, empowering each model
to shine authentically and break barriers in the fashion industry.

Our collaborative ethos extends beyond the confines of our London headquarters, as we embark on four annual
tours to Italy, the epicenter of global fashion. These immersive experiences not only showcase the unparalleled
talent of our models but also foster meaningful connections with international designers and industry insiders.
